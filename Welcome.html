CAD.io is a free online MRI viewing and AI driven diagnostic tool that aims to increase physician efficiency in developing countries
According to The Application of Medical Artificial Intelligence Technology in Rural Areas of Developing Countries some countries have as few as one radiologist for every 2 million people. The goal of this project is to provide these radiologists with free tools to improve their workflow and increase the number of patient's they can treat.
This project was created for my college capstone class and has become a personal project to demonstrate my abilities.
